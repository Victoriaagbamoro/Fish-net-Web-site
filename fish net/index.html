Fish Net

Connecting You to Local Fish Farmers

Fish net is a socio-economic enterprise that is focused on helping low-income fish mongers in less developed communities. By creating and connecting these sellers to a wider market outside their respective communities using technology, thereby relieving them of the stress of delivering their goods to other marketers and consumers.
